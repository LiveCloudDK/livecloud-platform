Security & compliance

Verified guests. Secure tickets. Your data.

LiveCloud builds security in at the foundation, not on top: verified identities, verified transfers, and data that stays yours.

Every guest is verified with MitID (Denmark's national digital ID), both on profile creation and whenever tickets or single-day access is transferred between users. That eliminates scalping and fake tickets, and ensures every ticket ends up with a verified person — not in a shadow market.

All data belongs to the festival, not to us. Data is stored securely in an EU data centre. We work in accordance with GDPR, and DPA and sub-processor agreements are in place and ready as part of onboarding.

The platform is our own, from core to API, with no dependency on third-party suppliers. That means transparent pricing, clear lines of responsibility, and one partner to talk to — no grey zones about who owns what.

All payments are handled through established partners such as Swedbank and certified acquirers. We don't handle card data ourselves; we build on top of proven payment infrastructure.

What this gives you
  • MitID on all profiles and transfers — scalping shut down
  • All data belongs to you, stored in an EU data centre
  • DPA and sub-processor agreements ready from day one
  • No third-party suppliers to coordinate
  • Transparent pricing, one partner
  • Stable, certified payment handling
  • Clear lines of responsibility for audits
Why we build it this way

We start from what guests already know.

Digital self-service is a natural part of everyday life. Net-banking, MitID, Apple Wallet, MobilePay, mobile tickets and membership apps. They're habits most Danes carry on autopilot.

Those are exactly the patterns LiveCloud builds on.

Instead of introducing something new, we reuse what guests already understand. When they open the app, it feels familiar. Login, ticket, payment and overview sit where they expect. That cuts friction from the first tap.

For you as organiser, that means high adoption from day one. The app becomes the natural gathering point for the guest — ticket, Beats, programme and communication, all in one place.

The result is more streamlined operations, better flow on the grounds, and a stronger data foundation for optimising the experience and the revenue.

Guests who, exceptionally, don't use the app are handled simply via NFC wristbands from the Service Desk. They become part of the same system and the same overview — no separate processes.

The outcome is one unified platform, where every guest is included and where you as organiser have full control.
